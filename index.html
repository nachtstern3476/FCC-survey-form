Raden Adjeng Kartini

A prominent Indonesian activist who advocated for women's rights and female education.

About R.A Kartini

  • Fullname Raden Ajeng Kartini Djojo Adhiningrat
  • Born 21 April 1879 in Jepara, Indonesia
  • Dies at the age 25 on 17 September 1904, in Kabupaten Rembang, Indonesia
  • Known for his actions in fighting for women's rights and women's education
  • Inspired by R.A. Kartini's example, the Van Deventer family established the R.A. Kartini Foundation which built schools for women, starting in 1912 with 'Kartini's Schools' in Semarang, and followed by other women's schools in Surabaya, Yogyakarta, Malang, Madiun, Cirebon, and other areas
